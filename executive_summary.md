## Recommendations

### Immediate Actions
- Review signal timing at high-congestion intersections
- Investigate queue formation patterns during peak hours

### Medium-Term Improvements
- Consider adaptive signal control during peak periods
- Implement weekend-specific timing plans

### Data Quality
- Schedule maintenance for detectors showing flat signals
- Continue monitoring data quality metrics
